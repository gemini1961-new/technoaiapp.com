How to use

  • 1. First step, download the Socratic app from either the Google Play or Apple App Store. The power of AI-based learning is now just a tap away!
  • 2. Once installed, launch the Socratic App. On opening, you’ll be greeted with a camera screen. The simplicity of the interface is beauty in itself; only two buttons, one for capturing the image and the other to toggle flash.
  • 3. Simply point your device’s camera at the homework question that needs solving. Snap the shot. Socratic will run its AI magic to analyse and understand the problem.
  • 4. After a moment, you’ll be presented with a detailed, step-by-step solution to the captured query. It also provides additional learning resources to exert a deeper understanding of the topic.
  • 5. If you ever wish to review past questions, Socratic stores your history. Stuck on a problem? Check your archive!
