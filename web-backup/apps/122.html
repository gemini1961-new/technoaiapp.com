How to use

  • 1. Access the Nova AI chatbot through our website or mobile application. Select 'Start Chat' to begin.
  • 2. You can start by typing 'Hi Nova' or any greeting of your choice. Remember, Nova is all about mimicking human-like interaction, so decorum counts!
  • 3. Ask Nova anything, from daily weather updates to cooking recipes. The more diverse your questions are, the more Nova learns!
  • 4. Should you need support or guidance on specific topics, just ask! Nova is designed to offer help in various areas.
  • 5. If you had a long day and need some cheering up, try asking Nova to tell you a joke. Laughter, after all, is the best AI therapy!
