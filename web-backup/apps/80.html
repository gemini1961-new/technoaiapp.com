How to use

  • 1. To get started with Finch 3D, first, download and install the software from the official Finch 3D website. Make sure your device meets the system requirements.
  • 2. Once installed, open Finch 3D and explore its user-friendly interface. You will notice different tools including those for sculpting, painting, rigging, and animating. Familiarize yourself with these tools.
  • 3. To create a 3D object, select 'Create' from the menu and pick a shape from the drop-down list. Try to experiment with the tools and effects to manipulate the shape.
  • 4. For more detailed designs, you can use the 'Sculpt' and 'Paint' features. These will allow you to add texture and color to your 3D model.
  • 5. When you're satisfied with your creation, head to the 'Render' tab. Here, you can adjust the lighting and resolution before finalizing your 3D masterpiece.
