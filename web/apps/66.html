Whether you're a budding bard seeking an A-plus sonnet for your next literature class, a published poet in need of a creative crutch, or even an absolute beginner who doesn’t know where to start, Google Bard has got your back. How to use

  • 1. The first step is as simple as visiting the Google Bard website. All you need is an internet-enabled device, a stable connection, and you are good to go.
  • 2. Next, you will find a prompt asking for keywords or phrases. Think about the theme, mood, or subjects you want to explore in your poem. Input as many or as few terms as you wish.
  • 3. Once you are done, click on the 'Generate' button. Here comes the fun part! Google Bard will now channel its AI magic to craft a unique poem for you, based on your chosen keywords.
  • 4. Don’t worry if the first attempt doesn’t resonate with you. Feel free to tweak the keywords or add new ones. Remember, creativity is a process of refinement.
