How to use

  • 1. To get started with INK, simply download and install the desktop app. Launch the application and set up your workspace for a clutter-free writing environment.
  • 2. Begin by typing in your content, or importing existing content into INK. As you type, you'll start noticing real-time SEO suggestions appearing on the right side of your work page.
  • 3. Pay attention to these suggestions. They hold the power to take your content from the shadows into the limelight. Using the keyword optimizer, build your content around high-performing keywords.
  • 4. Next, you'll encounter readability check, a feature that will reform your convoluted sentences into crisp, concise ones that are more reader-friendly.
  • 5. Save your work within the app, or export it in multiple file formats, the choice is yours. Before you know it, you'll be creating stellar, SEO-compliant content in no time.
