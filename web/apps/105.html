How to use

  • 1. To begin with, simply log in to the Writer dashboard using your registered email.
  • 2. Once logged in, you will land on a page where you can either start writing instantly or upload the document you want to edit. Writer supports a wide variety of formats.
  • 3. As you write or paste your content, Writer begins working. It corrects grammar errors, checks for readability, suggests synonyms to improve language quality, and even predicts and completes your sentences.
  • 4. Then, it helps adjust the tone, so your content matches your audience. Business, academic, casual – Writer has you covered.
  • 5. Lastly, save your work. Writer provides the option to save your work within the dashboard or you can download it. It’s your ally in the writing process, so use it as fits you best.
