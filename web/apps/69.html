How to use

  • 1. Remove the Train Engine from its packaging. Be careful not to force or rush this step; we wouldn't want any accidental scratches on your brand new engine.
  • 2. Introduce the Train Engine to your child. Let them observe, touch, and familiarize themselves with the toy to make them more comfortable with it.
  • 3. Set up your train tracks. You can create elaborate layouts or keep it simple — there is no wrong way to play, as long as you're having fun.
  • 4. Place the Train Engine on the tracks. Make sure the wheels are properly aligned with the track.
