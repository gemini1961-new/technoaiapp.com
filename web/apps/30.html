How to use

  • 1. Make your way to the ThumbnailAi official website and charm yourself with the user-friendly interface. No extra clutter, just what you need.
  • 2. After logging in to your account, select the 'Create Thumbnail' option. If you don't have an account, don't panic! Clicking 'Sign Up' should set you up in no time.
  • 3. You'll then get to choose your preferred layout, add images and text. ThumbnailAi is also pretty good at giving suggestions. So, if you're having a creativity block, fear not!
  • 4. Once you're happy with your design, simply hit 'Generate Thumbnail'. Wait, and voila! Your masterpiece is ready to conquer the digital world.
  • 5. For quick, hassle-free downloads, click 'Download Thumbnail'. You can either download it directly or share the link. Yes, it's that easy to shine with ThumbnailAi.
