How to use

  • 1. After launching Code Snippets, you will notice a simple and clean interface. On it, there's an option to 'Add New Snippet'. Click on it to get started.
  • 2. A text box will appear where you can type in or paste your code. You also have an option to tag and categorize your snippet for future easy accessibility.
  • 3. Once you're finished editing, just hit 'Save'. Your snippet is now stored and ready for your next project.
  • 4. Want to use your saved snippet? Simply go to the 'My Snippets' section, locate your saved snippet and with a simple click, it will be inserted in your current project.
  • 5. With Code Snippets, you have total control and can modify or delete any snippet in a jiffy. Go to 'My Snippets', find the one you wish to alter, make changes, and hit 'Save'. Voila! Easier than making toast.
