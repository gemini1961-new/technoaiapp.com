How to use

  • 1. Getting started with Monica is a breeze. Simply sign up with an active email ID, create your password and voila – you are onboard!
  • 2. Once signed in, you can create profiles for all your contacts. You can fill in the essential details - birthdays, anniversaries, addresses, and even small details like favorite food or color. The more you append in Monica, the less you forget!
  • 3. Sending birthday wishes or remembering anniversaries just got easier. Monica will remind you of important dates and events.
  • 4. Monica offers a feature to track the conversations you've had with your contacts. After a conversation, you can make notes about it in the respective person's profile. It helps you to recall the small details that can have significant impacts on your relations.
  • 5. Last but not least, Monica comes with a user-friendly interface meaning you can easily search for contacts and update details. Managing relationships was never this easy and efficient!
