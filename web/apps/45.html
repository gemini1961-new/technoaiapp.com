How to use

  • 1. To get started, just type an author's name or book title into the Litmaps search bar. The engine will generate a 'map' of literary connections, spun from the data of countless resources.
  • 2. Click on any node in your map to delve deeper. You'll get a brief introduction to the author or book, along with a chart of related works.
  • 3. Literature is a conversation— and Litmaps helps you join in! Use the 'Discussion' function to share your thoughts or learn new perspectives. Analyze, speculate, and connect with fellow enthusiasts across the globe
  • 4. Interested in a particular literary era, movement or country? Use the filters to narrow down your search. Whether you're a fan of French Romanticism or Postmodern American Fiction, Litmaps will guide you to the right place!
