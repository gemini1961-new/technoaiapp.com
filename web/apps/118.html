How to use

  • 1. First, download the Supernormal app available on both Android and iOS platforms. Installation is a cinch, just follow the prompts.
  • 2. Upon successful installation, open the app to start setting up your account. Provide a valid email address and decide on a password, you'll use these to log in to your account.
  • 3. Welcome aboard! You're now on the main page. You can create your tasks by clicking on the 'Add Task' button. Input the details of the task, including the title, deadline and any necessary notes.
  • 4. You may also categorize your tasks according to different projects, clients, or areas of life. You can do this by clicking on 'New Project' and assigning tasks to it.
  • 5. Set your priorities right. Supernormal allows you to organize your tasks based on their urgency or importance. Just click on the task and adjust its priority level.
  • 6. Lastly, check off your tasks as you complete them. With Supernormal, consistency is key. Keep ticking off tasks and watch your productivity skyrocket!
