How to use

  • 1. To begin your journey with DeepSwap, visit the DeepSwap website and create a free account.
  • 2. Choose the images in which you wish to replace a face. Make sure they are clear and high quality for the best result.
  • 3. Upload the desired images onto the platform and select the face you want to replace. You can replace up to 5 faces at a time in a single photo!
  • 4. Click on ‘swap faces’ and the impressive AI algorithm will take over from here, creating a hyper-realistic swap. Wait as the magic occurs in few split seconds.
  • 5. Voila! Your image is ready to awe and amaze! Download or directly share in your preferred social media channels. Stun the masses with the sublimity of DeepSwap.
