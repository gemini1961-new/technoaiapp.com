How to use

  • 1. Kick-off your Notion AI journey by signing up for an account on the website. With a quick sign-up process, you're just a few clicks away from organized bliss!
  • 2. Once you're all signed in, you'll be presented with a user-friendly dashboard. Here, you'll find options to create notes, tasks, and projects. You can also customize the view to suit your preferences - simplicity at its finest.
  • 3. To make notes, simply click on the 'New Note' option and start scribbling away. The AI will categorize and organize your notes automatically!
  • 4. Use the task and project management features to keep track of all your ongoing work. Click on 'New Task' or 'New Project', input the relevant details, and voila! Notion AI takes care of all the organization, leaving you more time to focus on your work.
  • 5. Lastly, don't forget to explore the intelligent learning feature. As you continue to use Notion AI, it will start learning your habits and preferences, further personalizing the experience for you.
