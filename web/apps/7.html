How to use

  • 1. Sign Up: Start off your organization journey by creating an account in Briefly. Fill out the required information to set up your personal dashboard.
  • 2. Dashboard Overview: Find all your tools at your fingertips on the personal dashboard. You can view and edit tasks, organize projects, and set reminders and deadlines.
  • 3. Add Tasks and Notes: Select the 'New Task' or 'New Note' option to get started. Enter the details, set reminders or deadlines, and add them to your dashboard.
  • 4. Manage Tasks: Update the progress of your tasks by marking them as in-progress, completed, or pending. This way, you'll always have an organized view of your responsibilities.
  • 5. Team Integration: If you work in a team, simply invite your mates to join Briefly and assign them tasks or projects. Track the progress and stay synced altogether!
