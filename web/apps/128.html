This amiable AI-powered writing assistant is your knight in shining pixels, swooping in to save your works from the jaws of typing errors, grammatical nuisances, and punctuation pitfalls - this is Batman for your Words! How to use

  • 1. Head to Grammarly.com and sign up for free or opt for a premium plan for wider features.
  • 2. Install your Grammarly extension to your Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox browser. It's like getting a handy-dandy grammar sidekick!
  • 3. Now, whenever you type anything on your browser, Grammarly will automatically check your text, underlining any errors.
  • 4. To correct errors, simply hover your cursor over the underlined parts and click on the suggested corrections. Voila, mistakes be gone!
  • 5. Additionally, you can use the Grammarly Editor by pasting or typing your text in it, and watch as it meticulously analyses your words.
  • 6. If you’re more of a Microsoft Office person, no worries! Grammarly can be integrated into MS Word and Outlook as well.
