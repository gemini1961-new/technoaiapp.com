How to use

  • 1. To get started with the Wonder Studio, first register and create an account on our platform. Profile set-up is as breezy as a walk in the park.
  • 2. Once logged in, you will find a varied array of tools on your dashboard. Familiarise yourself with these tools by practising on our demo templates.
  • 3. Commence your creative journey by selecting a project or template to work on. You can either choose from our rich library of pre-loaded designs or start from scratch.
  • 4. Use the different editing tools to customize your project. From filters, color schemes, to typography-Wonder Studio has got you covered.
  • 5. Now that your project seems ready, it’s time to preview it. Ensure everything is as per your liking.
  • 6. Once satisfied, save your project and publish it. Voila, you've successfully created an art piece using Wonder Studio!
