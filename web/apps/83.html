How to use

  • 1. Start by visiting the IconifyAI website and signing up for an account. It's simple and seamless, all you need is a valid email address.
  • 2. After your account is set up, dive into the dashboard. This is where you view your analytics, create tasks, and manage your projects. Navigate around the dashboard to familiarize yourself with the features.
  • 3. Begin by creating your first task. Select the 'Create Task' button, enter a relevant name and customize your task as needed. Click 'Create' at the end.
  • 4. You can easily monitor your tasks in the 'Task Management' section. You can also modify, update, or delete tasks here.
