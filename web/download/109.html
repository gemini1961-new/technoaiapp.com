  • 1. To begin your Frase experience, simply log into your account, or sign up if you're a first-timer. With a user-friendly interface, you'll be feeling like a pro in no time!
  • 2. Next, navigate to the Content Brief tab. Here's where the magic happens folks! Simply enter the topic of your upcoming masterpiece and voila! Frase will provide a comprehensive brief with suggestions for headings, keywords, and even length guidelines.
  • 3. Now, let's optimize some content, shall we? Click on the 'Optimize' button and feed your draft content into the Frase machine, and watch as it spits out an SEO score based on top-performing online articles.
