How to use

  • 1. Open the Dream Interpreter web page. A simple and user-friendly interface will welcome you.
  • 2. In the dedicated text box, type or paste a detailed and chronological description of your dream. The more detailed the description, the better the interpretation will be. Include feelings, colors, objects, and characters to create a full picture of your dream.
  • 3. Click 'Interpret My Dream'. Grab your cup of coffee while our AI system delves into analyzing the symbols, themes, and characters in your dream.
  • 4. Within moments, a personalized dream interpretation will appear on your screen. Read the analysis carefully and use it as a lens through which to understand your subconscious thoughts and feelings.
  • 5. Remember, Dream Interpreter is not an absolute source of truth but a guide to help you understand your dreams better. Always consider your personal context and feelings when interpreting your dreams.
