How to use

  • 1. To start off with Writerly, simply navigate to its website and sign up for an account. It's as easy as pumpkin pie.
  • 2. Once your account is set up, click on 'Create a new project.' Here, fill in the project's name and briefly describe what it's about. This will help Writerly understand your writing needs and style better.
  • 3. After setting up the project, you’ll get to the main writing space. This is where the magic happens! Start typing away. Suggestions, corrections, and enhancements pop up in real-time, assisting you in bringing the best out of your content.
  • 4. Writerly also offers different templates for various forms of writing. Whether it's a blog post, a screenplay, or a novel, there's a perfect template waiting just for you.
  • 5. Finally, once you're satisfied with your masterpiece, you can export your work. Writerly supports various formats like HTML, PDF, and more. Happy writing!
