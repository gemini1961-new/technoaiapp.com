How to use

  • 1. Start by feeding the Essay Builder with a basic essay brief or a handful of keywords. This helps the system understand the direction of your essay.
  • 2. Based on your input, the AI will generate an initial draft. Here, sharp sentences, cohesive paragraphs, and vivid language starts to unfold.
  • 3. If you're not satisfied with the initial draft, no worries! Essay Builder allows unlimited revisions. You can tweak, add, or erase any portion till it aligns with your vision.
  • 4. Among many neat features, Essay Builder also provides plagiarism checks and grammar corrections to avoid common essay pitfalls.
  • 5. Once you're happy with your final draft, just click on 'download' and voila! Your academic masterpiece is ready to impress.
