How to use

  • 1. Visit the AnswerThePublic website. You will be met by an elderly gentleman who seems impatient for your query - no pressure!
  • 2. Start by inserting your topic of interest into the search bar and select the desired country for your research, then hit the 'Get Questions' button.
  • 3. Sit back, relax and let AnswerThePublic do all the work. In no time, you'll be presented with a mind map laden with keyword suggestions.
  • 4. Now you can analyze the visually represented data and discover what the world has been searching about your topic of choice. Each branch on the mind map represents a question or a query string associated with the input keyword.
  • 5. Go beyond the visuals by exploring the 'Data' tab. Here, you'll find the same questions and queries, neatly listed and categorized for a more straightforward look.
  • 6. Once you've soaked in all the knowledge, simply export the data in a CSV file format. So, the next time you need a hand with content creation or keyword research, look no further than AnswerThePublic. Let it be your trusty guide in the ever-changing landscape of public interest.
