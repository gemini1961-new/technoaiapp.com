How to use

  • 1. Start by downloading the Cutout Pro software onto your device. Follow the instructions on your screen to install and setup.
  • 2. Launch Cutout Pro and open the image you want to edit. You can do this by clicking on 'File' and then 'Open', or by dragging and dropping the image file into the software.
  • 3. To remove a background, select the 'Magic Cutout' tool. Use your cursor to mark the area you want to remove, then click apply.
  • 4. You can also use the 'Manual Cutout' tool for more precision. This is particularly useful for complex images where the object and background colors are similar.
  • 5. Once satisfied with the cutout, save your work. Remember to choose high-quality settings for the best results.
  • 6. Last but not least, explore other features of Cutout Pro, such as layers, effects and filters for more creative fun. Enjoy the process!
