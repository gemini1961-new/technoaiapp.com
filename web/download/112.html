How to use

  • 1. Getting started is a breeze. Create an account on Notion AI. You'll be prompted to input some basic personal information to customize your experience.
  • 2. Once logged in, you'll see different modules to manage tasks, calendars, notes, and more. Feel free to explore, you won't break anything
  • 3. To add a new task or note, just hit the '+' symbol and begin typing. The AI will anticipate and adapt to your needs, making suggestions as you type.
  • 4. Use the search bar to find old notes or upcoming tasks. With the calendaring feature, you can easily visualize your schedule and make a plan.
  • 5. When you’re unsure about what to do, don’t hesitate to use the 'Help' feature. This amazing wonder tool- Notion AI, will guide you no matter what.
  • 6. Last but not least, remember to have fun! Unleash the power of your mind with Notion AI and let the sky be the limit.
