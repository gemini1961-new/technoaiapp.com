How to use

  • 1. Getting started with Synthesia is straightforward. Firstly, you need to sign up and create an account.
  • 2. Then, you can navigate to the dashboard where you’ll see a 'Create Project' button. Simply click on it to start your exciting video making journey.
  • 3. After creating your project, you will be able to access a variety of personalizable avatars. Choose one and start typing in your script. The software will transform the text into speech, and your avatar will act it out.
  • 4. Once you are satisfied with the avatar's performance, click on the 'Generate Video' button, and voila, your AI-powered video is ready to share with the world! Remember, Synthesia is here to make professional video production as easy as pie.
