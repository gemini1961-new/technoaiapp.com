How to use

  • 1. Launch your favorite browser and visit the OpenAI page hosting Chat GPT.
  • 2. Type what you want to say in the prompt and hit enter. Chat GPT: Open AI will respond shortly with its input.
  • 3. Interact naturally - there's no need for specific commands or complex instructions. It understands language holistically and responds as if human!
  • 4. To cease the chat, simply close the chat window, and remember - everything you chat will be completely anonymous and can't be traced back to you!
