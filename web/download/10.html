How to use

  • 1. Start by firing up the ChatPDF software on your device. Its user-friendly interface is sure to welcome you into a world of document conversion.
  • 2. Choose the 'Convert Chat to PDF' option to initiate the process. Brace yourself as the software gets ready to harness its powerful technology.
  • 3. Select the chat you wish to convert and click on 'Convert'. Yes, it's that simple! As you wait, ChatPDF works its magic behind the scenes to bring your PDF to life.
  • 4. Once the conversion process is complete, you’re presented with your fresh-off-the-press PDF document. You can easily download the document, share it, or have it printed for a trip down the memory lane or streamlined professional discussions.
