How to use

  • 1. Go to Lalal.ai website and you will find a very user-friendly interface to start with.
  • 2. Click on the 'Select Audio File' button and upload the file that needs to be separated. It accepts all common audio formats.
  • 3. Choose the process intensity level. Lalal.ai's engine comes with two processing options: Mild (which outputs two stems, namely, vocals and instrumental) and Aggressive (which outputs five stems i.e., vocals, drums, bass, piano, and other).
  • 4. Click on 'Process the File', and Lalal.ai uses its sophisticated AI technology to separate the tracks.
  • 5. Once processed, download the separate tracks back to your system. Voila! It's that simple to use Lalal.ai.
