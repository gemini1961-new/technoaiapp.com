How to use

  • 1. Start with creating an account on Codium. It's a fairly simple and straight-forward process. After successful sign-up, log into your account.
  • 2. Now, you are all set to start using Codium. The homepage will serve as your launchpad, it's here where you can add, edit, or scan through your contacts.
  • 3. To add a new contact, click on 'Add Contact' and fill in the details. You can enter basic information like name, phone number, email, etc. But remember, Codium is way more than that. You can add custom tags and notes, which makes it easier to categorize and remember your contacts.
  • 4. If you want to edit an existing contact, just find the contact from your list and click on 'Edit'. Make any changes you want and hit 'Save'. Easy peasy lemon squeezy!
  • 5. Use the 'Search' tool to find your contacts swiftly. With just typing a part of the name or tag, all matching results will spring up in a jiffy. Codium has a knack for making things simple and snappy.
