How to use

  • 1. Start with signing up on the Ruru platform using an active email address. With this, you'll gain full access to all functionalities that Ruru offers.
  • 2. Upon logging in, tap on the dashboard to find the list of services provided by Ruru. Depending on your need, choose a service.
  • 3. To set a reminder, for instance, tap on the 'Reminder' service. Input the details about the reminder including time, date, and a little description of the task.
  • 4. Use the 'Schedule' function to arrange meetings, appointments or family events. Keypoint to note is that Ruru syncs these plans seamlessly with your online calendars.
  • 5. Got a lot on your plate? Fret not, Ruru also offers an email management functionality. Simply tap 'Manage Email' setting, and Ruru will efficiently sort your emails, highlighting the most crucial and arranging them for your perusal.
