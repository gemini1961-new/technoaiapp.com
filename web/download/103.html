How to use

  • 1. Start by logging into your Copy.ai account. If you don't have an account yet, simply visit Copy.ai's website and sign up for a free trial.
  • 2. Once you're in, navigate to the dashboard. Here, you'll find various content generation options to choose from. Depending on your need, you can select anything from brainstorming ideas to drafting full blog posts.
  • 3. After you've chosen the desired function, enter your input text into the provided field. This could be a product description, your latest marketing campaign idea, or even a blog post title. The key is to be as specific as possible to get the best results.
  • 4. Hit the 'Generate Content' button, sit back, and watch the magic happen. In mere moments, Copy.ai will provide you with a range of creative options where you could select or modify the content accordingly.
  • 5. Once satisfied, simply copy and paste the generated content into your desired destination. Whether it's an email draft, a social media caption, or a business proposal—the world is your oyster with Copy.ai!
